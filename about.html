About Us

What We Do



As a web development club at a college, we would focus on providing opportunities for members to learn and enhance their web development skills. This could involve organizing workshops, inviting guest speakers, and participating in coding challenges and hackathons. We would also encourage members to collaborate on web development projects.

Who We Are



As a web development club at NIT Rourkela, we are a group of enthusiastic students with a passion for coding and web development. Our club is made up of members from different departments and backgrounds who share a common interest in building websites and web applications. We are committed to fostering a supportive and inclusive community.

How We Work



At WebWiz, we work collaboratively and creatively to develop cutting-edge web solutions. We prioritize continuous learning and skill development by organizing regular coding workshops, coding challenges, and hackathons. Our members work in small teams to build websites and web applications for various on-campus organizations and local businesses.

What We Believe



At WebWiz, we believe in the transformative power of technology and the internet. We believe that web development is a crucial skill in today's digital world, and that it has the potential to positively impact society. We are passionate about creating websites and web applications that can solve real-world problems.
